Aurora – 2D Side Scrolling Platformer

This game is a unique side scrolling platformer in which the player navigates the level whilst collecting power ups and defeating enemies. The game utilises a life and health system that the player has to manage throughout the level paired with a variety of powerups and pickups the player can use to help complete the level. The use of Nav Mesh AI for the enemies provides an added challenge to the player in order to navigate the level they must defeat every enemy. Finally the use of uniquely created art, assets and animations also provides a more personalised theme to the game.

  • Unity
  • C#
